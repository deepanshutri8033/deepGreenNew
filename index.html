The Problem

Today, large corporations file misleading Sustainability Reports to save on taxes and improve their brand image. They claim to have planted thousands of trees, but in reality, those trees exist only on paper—a practice known as 'Phantom Forests'. DeepGreen directly targets this gap, verifying corporate claims against real-world satellite ground truth automatically.

100% Software-Based Audit Engine // AI vs Greenwashing

The Carbon Trust Deficit

Global carbon markets are currently plagued by 'phantom offsets' and untrusted data. Studies show that over half of active nature-based carbon credits do not represent genuine tree growth. DeepGreen solves this accountability gap by replacing slow, manual compliance audits with instantaneous, unalterable satellite telemetry.

Key Differentiators

& Core Features

↓

No Hardware Required

DeepGreen is a 100% software-based platform. We bypass expensive and hard-to-maintain physical ground sensors by pulling direct, open-source geospatial data from global satellite networks and automated web-scraping pipelines.

Confidence Scoring

Geospatial data is noisy. Our engine filters out cloud cover, shadows, and seasonal vegetation changes to calculate a Deforestation Confidence Score (0–100%), ensuring regulators only act on high-certainty violations.

Multi-Year Trend Analysis

Avoid false alarms from natural cycles. DeepGreen tracks 3–5 year historical NDVI trends, mapping true long-term deforestation versus cyclical seasonal agricultural patterns.

Explainability Layer

Every audit result is verifiable. We provide citation-style tracing that highlights the exact source paragraphs and pages from the corporate CSR report, building absolute trust by showing exactly how the AI reached its conclusion.

Automated Alerting

Immediate action on violation. When deforestation is detected, DeepGreen instantly generates a legal compliance PDF and dispatches alerts directly to government environmental regulators.
