DeepGreen replaces manual sustainability auditing with an automated pipeline. Our platform digests ESG reports, extracts corporate location assertions using **Multimodal RAG**, and cross-references those coordinates against multi-spectral satellite indices.

RAG Extraction + NDVI Computer Vision

The Science of NDVI Tracking

Vegetation reflects near-infrared light and absorbs red light. DeepGreen calculates the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) dynamically using multi-spectral bands. By tracking vegetative density fluctuations over a 3-5 year timeline, we can accurately identify deforestation alerts while ignoring natural seasonal patterns.
